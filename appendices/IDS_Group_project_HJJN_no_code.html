Traffic accident in Korean expressways

by Hyewon Kim, Jimin Ju, Jiweon Kim, and Nasimullah Latifi

2020. 7. 31

This report analyzes the Traffic accidents in 7 expressways which occurred from 2015 to 2019 in the region of Gwangju and Jeonnam province.

Traffic accidents patterns by year

The total number of accident in expressways from 2015 to 2019 is 810, and it is decreasing every year.

Traffic accident reasons per expressway

In most expressways, ‘distraction’ and ‘speeding’ were the most causes of accidents. However, in Muan-Gwangju expressway, accidents by short sight safety distance was uniquely high. Also Seohaean expressway shows the relatively high rate of accidents by road related problems.

Accidents by weather

We compared the total cases by weather in each highway. In general, people usually think that there are many accidents on rainy and cloudy days, but the result shows that most of accidents happened on sunny days.

Red color means Sunny day, and sunny day took the widest part of the pie chart that meant that there were the most accidents on sunny day. Especially “Muan-Gwangju” and “Seohaean” expressways occur traffic accident the most on sunny days.

Accidents by weekdays

We compared the total cases by weekdays. Following the graph, there were the most traffic accidents on Friday, and the second top was on Saturday. Therefore the more accidents occurred on weekend.We can easily distinguish the difference by treemap.

Accidents by age groups

In most cases, 40~60s were most frequent age groups of drivers of traffic accidents and those of 20~30s were followed. However, since 2015, the proportion of traffic accidents by people in their 60s and older has been on the rise, especially in 2019, which is a very high increase. So it is worth noting.
